Our Story

A Legacy of Compassionate Care

Founded in 2010, PawPrints Veterinary began with a simple mission: to provide the highest standard of veterinary medicine with a gentle, loving touch.

Over the years, we've grown from a small neighborhood clinic to a state-of-the-art medical facility, but our core values remain the same. We treat every pet as if they were our own, and every client like family.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our clinic.

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes, we are always happy to welcome new furry members to our clinic family! You can book your first appointment online or by calling our front desk.

Do you offer emergency services?

We provide urgent care during our regular business hours and partner with local emergency hospitals for 24/7 after-hours care.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and offer flexible payment plans through CareCredit to ensure your pet gets the care they need.

Can I tour the facility?

Absolutely! We'd love to show you around. Give us a call to schedule a brief introductory tour of our state-of-the-art clinic.
